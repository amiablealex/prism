◆ Prism Wars ◆

Bend light, control territory, dominate the board

How to Play

⚡

Energy System

10 energy per turn. Place pieces (2-8 energy) or pick up your pieces (1 energy)

🪞

Mirrors (8)

Reflect light at 90°. Cost: 2 energy. Your most efficient tool

✂️

Splitters (3)

Split into 2 perpendicular beams. Cost: 4 energy. Precise control

◆

Prism (1)

3 beams for massive coverage. Cost: 8 energy. Use wisely - only 1!

⬛

Blockers (4)

Completely stop light. Cost: 3 energy. Can't place near sources

🔄

Pickup & Replace

Pick up your pieces (1 energy) and reuse them strategically

🌟

Amplifiers

Golden 2x tiles. Territory here counts double!

🎯

Objectives

Complete 2 secret objectives. Recalculated each turn!

⚡

Combos

Chain 3+ mirrors or use prisms well for bonus points

🏆

Victory

First to 75 points or highest after 15 rounds wins!

👁️

Visual Controls

Toggle beams, particles, and players to reduce visual chaos

🔌

Auto-Reconnect

Refresh or disconnect? You can rejoin automatically!
