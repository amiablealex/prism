◆ Prism Wars ◆

Bend light, control territory, dominate the board

How to Play

🪞

Mirrors

Reflect light beams at 90° angles. Your most versatile tool (15 available)

✂️

Splitters

Split light into 2 perpendicular beams. Precise area control (6 available)

◆

Prisms

Split light into 3 beams for massive coverage. Use wisely - only 1 per player!

⬛

Blockers

Stop enemy light. Light penetrates first blocker, stopped by second (8 available)

🌟

Amplifiers

Golden 2x tiles randomly placed. Territory on these tiles counts double!

🎯

Objectives

Complete secret objectives for bonus points. Control corners, edges, or spread territory

⚡

Combos

Chain 3+ mirrors or use prisms effectively to earn combo bonuses

🏆

Victory

First to 75 points wins! Points = Territory + Objectives + Combos
